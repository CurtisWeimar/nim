Game of Nim
Play
Rules

Nim is a game where two players alternately take turns in taking redstone torches from several rows. The only rule is that each player must take at least one torch on their turn, but they may take more than one torch in a single turn, as long as they all come from the same row. The player who takes the last remaining torch on the entire board loses.
